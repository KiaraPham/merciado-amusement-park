Interactive dark ride game experience

An all-weather incredible multi-sensory walk in attraction no bookings required. If you are over 5 years old and 102cm you can strap yourself into futuristic motion seats, take aim with the world's fastest electric targeting guns, immerse yourself in virtual 3D movies competing against fellow riders to see who the best shot is! Theres something to suit almost everyone, a range of movie rides awaits. Darkrides high tech motion seats which generate up to 400 movements per second and up to 2Gs acceleration creating sensations of falling, twisting, flying, turning, accelerating, bumps, jumps and even floating through multiple interactive adventures. Darkride will have you screaming, giggling and coming back for more. We'll capture your ride thrills with your score on a photo so you can share the excitement.

Tilt-A-Whirl

Which dip or hill will send your car into a spinning frenzy? Travel around an undulating track on a free spinning, whipping, and whirling joyride.

Spinning Teacups

Spin ‘round and ‘round in an oversized teacup during a madcap music-filled adventure.
